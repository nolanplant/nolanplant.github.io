Neck Protocol · Every Session · 5 min
1
Chin Tucks
Pull chin straight back (double chin). Against wall for feedback. Hold 5 sec.
15 reps
2
Neck Nods
Slow, controlled yes-motion. Against gravity, not momentum.
15 reps
3
Suboccipital Release
Hands behind skull, gently nod chin down and hold.
30s hold
4
Platysma Activation
Open mouth wide, pull corners of lips down hard. Hold 10 sec.
10 reps
Posture reminder: Tongue on roof of mouth (behind teeth), lips closed, teeth lightly touching. Do this constantly — desk, car, surfing.
Golden Rules
Progressive overload: Add reps or weight every week. Hit the top of the rep range easily? Go heavier next session.

Surf days: Surf counts. On heavy surf days cut to neck protocol + one superset only.

Fasted training: Ideal to train before first meal. Coffee is fine. Boosts fat oxidation.

Protein first: Every meal starts with protein. Hit 0.8–1g per lb of bodyweight daily.
